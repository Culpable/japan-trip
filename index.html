Before we fly

Trip prep, together

    01 / CHINA

    Hong Kong & Shenzhen

    01–05 AUG
    01SAT · AUG

    Arrive Hong Kong → cross to Shenzhen

    • CX 184 · Perth T1 → Hong Kong · 8:20AM–4:20PM
    • Cross Lo Wu or Shenzhen Bay border into China
    • Set up DiDi, WeChat Pay, Alipay and Meituan
    • Check in and explore the local neighbourhood
    02SUN · AUG

    Huaqiangbei & Shenzhen city life

    • Explore the Huaqiangbei electronics markets
    • Try Meituan drone food delivery
    • Visit Futian CBD and the Nanshan tech area
    • Browse wet markets and local food halls
    03MON · AUG

    Day trip to Guangzhou Circle

    • High-speed rail from Shenzhen to Guangzhou, about 55 minutes
    • See the 138-metre coin-shaped tower on the Pearl River
    • Stay for dusk photography and the figure-eight reflection
    • Return to Shenzhen in the evening

    The Guangzhou Circle is in Guangzhou, not Shenzhen. The high-speed rail is the practical route.

    04TUE · AUG

    Final Shenzhen day → Hong Kong

    • Final Shenzhen shopping and street food
    • Cross back into Hong Kong
    • Stay near the airport for the early departure

    CX 450 departs at 9:55AM tomorrow. Aim to reach the airport by 7:30AM.

    02 / JAPAN

    Tokyo

    05–09 AUG
    05WED · AUG

    Fly to Tokyo & first night in Ginza

    • CX 450 · Hong Kong T1 → Narita T2 · 9:55AM–5:15PM
    • Check in and pick up a Suica card at Narita
    • Explore Ginza and the Dior Ginza boutique
    • Make the first Lawson or 7-Eleven run

    You arrive at Narita T2 but depart from T1 on 15 August.

    06THU · AUG

    Dior Café → teamLab Planets

    • Dior Café at 10:30AM
    • Travel across Tokyo to Toyosu after lunch
    • teamLab Planets admission from 4:30PM to 5:00PM

    Dior Café · Thursday 6 August 2026 · 10:30AM · Open reservation

    teamLab Planets · Thursday 6 August 2026 · admission 4:30–5:00PM

    07FRI · AUG

    West Tokyo, Shibuya & Shinjuku

    • Rent bikes at Showa Kinen Park in Tachikawa
    • Ride a swan boat at Inokashira Park
    • Mario Kart street racing with an international driving permit
    • Shibuya Sky at sunset
    • See the 3D cat billboard and Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building
    • Visit a game centre and Cafe Reissue for 3D latte art

    Shibuya Sky timed entry · Cafe Reissue at opening

    08SAT · AUG

    Cultural Tokyo: Asakusa to Akasaka

    • Art Aquarium Museum in Nihonbashi
    • Try a walk-in at Pokémon Cafe in Nihonbashi
    • Ninja Restaurant dinner in Akasaka

    Pokémon Cafe is booked out online · try a walk-in on Saturday 8 August

    Ninja Restaurant · Saturday 8 August 2026

    03 / FUJI

    Fuji

    09 AUG ONLY
    09SUN · AUG

    Mt Fuji views → overnight

    • Mt Fuji views around the Five Lakes area
    • Choose a Fuji-area stay with an onsen for the night
    • Prepare for an early departure toward Kyoto
    04 / KANSAI

    Kyoto & Nara

    10–13 AUG
    10MON · AUG

    Start Kyoto: Shinkansen & Gion

    • Travel to Kyoto by Shinkansen from the Fuji area
    • Reserve a Fuji-facing seat where the route allows
    • Check in and wander Nishiki Market
    • Explore Gion at dusk and the stone-paved lanes

    Revised: 10 August now begins the Kyoto and Nara section. There is no Hakone or Fuji sightseeing today.

    11TUE · AUG

    Kyoto: kimono, bamboo & torii gates

    • Reach Arashiyama Bamboo Grove before 8:00AM
    • Explore Tenryu-ji and the Arashiyama riverside
    • Kimono and yukata rental at Kimono Miyabi Kyoto at 10:00AM
    • Cross Kyoto to Fushimi Inari
    • Walk through the torii gates, allowing about two hours

    Kimono Miyabi Kyoto · Tuesday 11 August 2026 · 10:00AM

    Arashiyama is in northwest Kyoto and Fushimi Inari is southeast. An early start keeps the route comfortable.

    12WED · AUG

    Nara: deer, temples & giant Buddha

    • Take the morning train from Kyoto to Nara, about 40–50 minutes
    • Feed the bowing deer in Nara Park
    • Visit Tōdai-ji and the Great Buddha Hall
    • Return to Kyoto or continue toward Osaka in the evening

    Splitting Nara into its own day removes the original three-city rush and gives each place proper time.

    05 / KANSAI

    Osaka & Koyasan

    13–15 AUG
    13THU · AUG

    Universal Studios & Osaka night

    • Universal Studios Japan and Super Nintendo World
    • Umeda Sky Building at night if energy allows
    • Ride the Don Quijote Ferris wheel
    • Dotonbori street food and takoyaki crawl

    USJ Express Pass and Super Nintendo World entry sell out months ahead

    14FRI · AUG

    Koyasan day trip → return to Tokyo

    • Visit the sacred Buddhist mountain town
    • Walk Okunoin Cemetery beneath the cedar forest
    • See Kongobuji Temple and Konpon Daito pagoda
    • Leave by 2:00PM and travel back to Tokyo to pack

    Nankai Koya Line from Osaka Namba plus cable car takes about 1.5 hours each way.

    15SAT · AUG

    Tokyo → Perth via Ho Chi Minh City

    • VN 307/791 · Narita T1 → Ho Chi Minh City → Perth · 9:30AM–10:30PM
    • Leave the hotel by about 5:30AM
    • Check in at Narita Terminal 1
    • Allow for the six-hour and six-hour-ten-minute flight sectors

    The free T1–T2 shuttle takes about 15 minutes. Check-in opens roughly three hours before departure.
